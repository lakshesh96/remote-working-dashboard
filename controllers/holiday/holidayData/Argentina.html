Coronavirus: Travel status Argentina has extended its entry ban as part of the national quarantine that began on March 20th. Only flights carrying cargo and those involved in the repatriation of citizens will be allowed to operate, the government said. Further, only citizens and permanent residents of Argentina are permitted to enter the country and are subject to a 14-day quarantine.  Argentina Coronavirus Travel Guide
